Paragliding in Croatia

Paragliding

To be free like a bird, to become a friend with the air currents and caress the tops of the clouds are ideas as old as man is. People want to sample the heights. People love to fly. With aeroplanes, balloons, kites… and when they decide to combine parachuting and sailing? Then there comes paragliding.

This is a relatively young sport which in Croatia has found a perfect partner. A partner for life. The spectacular landscapes around Biokovo and the views of Brač, Hvar and Hercegovina, the Kalnik take-off sites which even for the most experienced represent a challenge, friendly Tribalj where even an absolute beginner will feel safe plus the Istria’s Raspadalica where you will be able to almost touch the tops of Učka are destinations which are engraved in the memory of every fan of paragliding.

In order for you to become a son of the wind in Croatia, you only need light hiking boots, sports clothing, sunglasses, sun cream and goodwill. Just as with parachute jumps, this is about tandem paragliding when in the hands of experienced flyers, you will spend the day of your dreams. The procedure is very simple.
