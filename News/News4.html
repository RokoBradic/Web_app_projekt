SEA KAYAKING DUBROVNIK SUNSET

Kayaking in Dubrovnik

The Old Town of Dubrovnik might always be spectacular, but it is never more so than at sunset. With the sky lit up, the sea shimmering and the stars appearing, the town comes alive with the colors of the sunset.

Departing ~2.5 hours before sunset, let our expert, English-speaking guide take you on a kayaking exploration of the city’s Old Town walls, on the seas along the shores of the mysterious island of Lokrum and for a stop at a popular, but secluded beach cave on the mainland, before heading back towards the city as the sun goes down. Glide through sunset waters, take spectacular pictures and soak up the beauty from the tranquility of your kayak. Savor a glass of chilled, Croatian wine upon return to the beach.

Uncover tales of Dubrovnik’s history as you soak in the fresh evening air, ocean freedom and indomitable beauty of the cityscape. Adopt the invaders’ perspective paddling close to the battle-worn, medieval city walls. Admire Dubrovnik’s most glamorous neighbourhood and stop at Europe’s first quarantine zone.
